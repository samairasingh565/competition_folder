Indian Air Force

WELCOME!

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. Its complement of personnel and aircraft assets ranks third amongst the air forces of the world.Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and to conduct aerial warfare during armed conflict. Founded 26 January 1950; 72 years ago (as current service) 8 October 1932; 89 years ago (as Royal Indian Air Force) Country India Type Air force Role Aerial warfare Size 170,576 active personnel 140,000 reserve personnel Approx 1926+ aircraft Part of Indian Armed Forces Headquarters Integrated Defence Headquarters, Ministry of Defence, New Delhi Motto(s) नभः स्पृशं दीप्तम् (Sanskrit) Nabhaḥ Spr̥śaṁ Dīptam (ISO) transl. 

Training Sessions


NATIONAL DEFENCE ACADEMY After successful completion of three year training at NDA , cadets would go to Air Force Academy for Flying/Ground duty training of one year duration. On successful completion of training, they get commissioned as Permanent Commission Officers All Air Force training establishments are equipped with the state-of-the-art infrastructure and best of the facilities including institutes, swimming pools, squash courts etc. Once you join these training establishments, the IAF takes care of all your requirements like messing, lodging etc. and also gives you a monthly stipend during the last one year of training.



ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Eligibility Criteria

Air Force Group X & Y Eligibility Post Age Limit GROUP 'X' Trades (Except Education Instructor) 17-21 years of age GROUP 'X': Education Instructor Trade 20-25 years of age or 20-28 years of age depending on the subjects of the candidates GROUP 'Y' Trades (Except Med Asst and Musician) 17-21 years of age Eligibility Criteria to Get into Indian Air Force after 12th Candidates will have to satisfy the minimum eligibility requirement decided by the Union Public Service Commission( UPSC). Educational Requirements: Applicants must have cleared 12th with mathematics and physics as main subjects. Those who are appearing in the final year of examination may also apply. Age Limit: The age of candidates who are applying for the examination should be between 16½-19 years at the time of filling the application form. Gender: Only male candidates are eligible to apply for this examination.
